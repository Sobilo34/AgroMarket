About Us

Agrospace Nigeria is an online market confluence for farmers to sell farm produce to different customers at a good price. It enables the farmers to find their best business partner and sell at a good rate. Agrospace Nigeria offers more features like enabling investment in farm business, offering farming orientation for farmers, and building a community for buyers and sellers of farm produce.
